About Us

We are the students from Engineering who opted to start a webinar series for the students who are quite keen to know about the industrial life that awaits in our upcoming future. The team is lead by one of the best professors from Department of CSE Dr. Arindam Karmakar Sir. We wish to present some of the finest speakers who themselves are in that sector with a lot of experience in their respective fields.

Our Objectives


  • Innovative analysis of tech-trending topics by proficient speakers.
